Choose Your Own Adventure

Addendum

Process and Creative Choices: Creating the "Choose Your Own Adventure" game involved structuring a dynamic narrative with various paths and endings. The design choices were focused on simplicity and ease of interaction, ensuring users could easily follow the story and make choices without distraction. The narrative elements were complemented with relevant images to enhance the immersive experience.

Challenges Faced: One challenge was ensuring that the story transitions smoothly between different states, especially at the end of the game. To address this, we implemented an endGame function that updates the story text, removes choice buttons, and presents a restart option. Another challenge was styling the game to be both visually appealing and responsive across different devices, which required careful use of CSS transitions, hover effects, and media queries.

Insights Gained: This project provided insights into structuring interactive content in a way that keeps users engaged. The importance of visual feedback and smooth transitions became evident, as they significantly enhance user experience. Additionally, incorporating responsive design principles ensured that the game is accessible and enjoyable on both desktop and mobile devices.
